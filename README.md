## Bye GitHub, Hello Codeberg!

I am in the process of migrating my personal projects over to [Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/ngie) for personal/ethical reasons.

I will continue to have a presence on GitHub for the time being, but it will not be the primary source of truth for [F]OSS development I do.

The reasons for my migration to Codeberg align with other projects, such as [Gentoo Linux](https://www.gentoo.org/news/2026/02/16/codeberg.html) and [Zig](https://ziglang.org/news/migrating-from-github-to-codeberg/).
